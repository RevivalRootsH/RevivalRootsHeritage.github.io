About Alexander Township

Alexander Township, also known as Alex, is one of South Africa's oldest and most vibrant communities, located near Johannesburg. It is renowned for its historical significance and cultural richness, serving as a melting pot of South African heritage.

History of Alexander Township

Established in 1912, Alexander Township has been a center of resilience, struggle, and triumph during South Africa's apartheid era. It is home to notable figures such as Nelson Mandela, who lived here before moving to Soweto.

Tourist Attractions in Alexander Township

  • Mandela Yard: The former residence of Nelson Mandela during his time in Alex.
  • Alexandra Heritage Centre: A museum showcasing the history and cultural artifacts of the township.
  • Alexandra Township Art Scene: Experience vibrant street art and local crafts.
  • Gumede Park: A historic recreational area perfect for picnics and community events.
